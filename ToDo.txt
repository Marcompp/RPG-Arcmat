Dice game ideas:

Special face idea: the Escort ( E ) 
- forms a Group with every different non-numeric face present.  
Base Hands: 
-Two different non-numeric faces including E ex: E + X = Escort's Hand (above a Wanderer's Hand) (counts as a pair for the purposes of forming Double Duet, the Hearth, the Peregrine or the Dynastic Marriage) 
-Three different non-numeric faces including E ex: E + X + B = Manage au Trois (above Three Arcs) (counts as a trio for the purposes of forming the Hearth) 
-Four different non-numeric faces including E ex: E + X + B + S = Conspiracy (above Four Arcs) 
-Five different non-numeric faces including E ex: E + X + B + S + M = Baccanal (above Five Arcs) 

Crowned Variants: 
-Escort's Hand + 1 Crown = Crowned Escort's Hand (above Crowned Wanderer's Hand) 
-Escort's Hand + 2 Crowns = King's Meeting (above King's Wanderer) 
-Escort's Hand + 3 Crowns = Dynastic Marriage 
-Manage au Trois + 1 Crown = Crowned Threesome (above Crowned Three Arcs) 
-Manage au Trois + 2 Crowns = King's Threesome (above King's Three Arcs) 
-Conspiracy + 1 Crown = Crowned Conspiracy (above Crowned Four Arcs) 

Because Escort + Crown would technically be an Escort's Hand: 
-Escort + Crown = Crowned Escort (above Crowned Hand) 
-Escort + 2 Crowns = King's Escort (above King's Hand) 
-Escort + 3 Crowns = Courtesain (above Triumvirate) 
-Escort + 4 Crowns = Red Queen (above Four Day Reign) 

Because Escort + Graveyard overlaps with Escort's Hand: 
-Escort + 3 Skulls = Escort's Grave (above Graveyard) 
-Escort + 3 Skulls + Crown = Crowned Escort's Grave (above Crowned Graveyard) 
-Escort's Hand + Graveyard = The Widower (because Escort's Hand is counted as a pair)
